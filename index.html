Biography

David Sinden is a senior scientist at Fraunhofer Institute for Digital Medicine Mevis, in Bremen. His research interests include ultrasound modelling, thermal ablation and pharmacokinetic models. He works within the modelling and simulation and image-guided therapy groups.
