& \multicolumn{2}{l}{\em Integrability, localisation and bifurcation of a elastic conducting rod in a magnetic field} \\ %[1mm]
& \hspace{0.50cm}\begin{minipage}{14cm} The static configurations of a rod in a magnetic field were rederived as a Hamiltonian system with symmetry, and shown to be completely integrable.
